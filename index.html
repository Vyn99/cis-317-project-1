This is a website for cool facts about the football club Manchester United.

Most Premier League wins

Manchester United has won a total of 13 Premier League wins. More than any other English football team.

Originaly founded as Newton Health

In 1878 the football club was founded as Newton Health football club. It was not until 1902 that the club changed its name to Manchester United.

Often refered to as the "Red Devils"

In the 1930's English rugby club Salford had toured France wearing red shirts and were called "The Red Devils". In the 1960's Manchester United fans began calling Manchester United "The Red Devils" because of Manchesters red shirts.

The club plays at Old Tafford stadium

All Manchester home games are played at Old Tafford stadium. Its history for iconic football games has made it the ideal place for the rich lineage of Manchester United. It is also one of the biggest stadiums in England.

One of the greatest football club manger in histroy is Sir Alex Ferguson

Sir Alex Ferguson began managing Manchester United in 1986. While he was managing the the club, Manchester won 13 Premier League titles and two UEFA Champions League titles.

The "Busby Babes" era

The "Busby Babes" were young talented players that played for Manchester United during the 1950s. In 1958, the Munich air disater claimed the lives of eight player. Essentially ending the era of the "Busby Babes".
